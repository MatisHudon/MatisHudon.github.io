7 Sensing Software

Biography

Currently Research Engineer at 7 sensing software.

I was a Research Fellow at the Trinity College of Dublin. I worked with Professor Aljosa Smolic in the VSENSE team. My research interests were computer vision, acquisition, photorealistic and non-photorealistic rendering, data science and deep neural networks. During my PostDoc I mostly focused on 2D animation techniques.

I obtained my engineering Degree in Electronic (specialized in Multimedia Technologies) from ENSEIRB-MATMECA graduate school of engineering. I obtained my PhD at the University of Rennes 1 at IRISA Rennes France in collaboration with Technicolor Rennes France.
